Information Collection and Use

The Application automatically collects and processes specific data parameters required to deliver spaceflight telemetry and orbital tracking calculations. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

Location Services (GPS Data): The Application utilizes your device's automated location parameters to compute real-time satellite overhead passes, upcoming rocket launch schedules, and localized meteor shower observation windows. This location data is processed locally on your device or via real-time telemetry network queries. If you deny location access permissions, the Application relies strictly on manual city lookups inputted by you. Location parameters are not tracked in the background or stored permanently by the Service Provider.
